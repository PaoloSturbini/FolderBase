# Code of Conduct

FolderBase aims to maintain a welcoming, constructive and professional community.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing personal or sensitive information.
- Assume good faith while remaining open to correction.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, doxxing and publication of another person's private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported privately to **appsecurity@pst.my**. Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject contributions or restrict participation when necessary to protect the project community.
